Spicy Sweet Chili Sauce

Description

Sweet chili sauce is a thick, sweet and spicy sauce with a garlicky kick. You can see pieces of chili and minced garlic suspended in the sauce. This sacue is a classic condiment in Asian cuisine, especially Thai and Vietnamese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C rice vinegar
  • 3/4 C water
  • 4 oz white sugar (1/2 C + 2 tbs)
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbs soy sauce (or 1/2 tbs fish sauce)
  • 1/2 tbs cayenne pepper
  • 2.8 oz sambal oelek or a generous 2 1/2 tbs
  • 4 tsp cornflour/cornstarch (or substitute arrowroot powder)
  • 2 tbs water

Steps

  1. Add the vinegar, water, sugar, garlic cloves, soy sauce and cayenne pepper into a nonstick pan.
  2. Heat over medium heat while stirring, to dissolve the sugar. Increase the heat to medium-high and bring to a boil. Let simmer approximately two minutes.
  3. Add the samgbal oelek and mix through. Let it cook for a few minutes until its slightly thickened (2-5 mins).
  4. Mix the cornstarch and water into a smooth slurry and add this to the sauce as well.
  5. While stirring frequently (to prevent from burning at the bottom), simmer the sauce for a further 2 to 5 minutes or until it reaches a desired thickness.
  6. Pour into glass jars with airtight lids (or heated and sterilized lids to store longer).
  7. Cool to room temp and store in fridge.
